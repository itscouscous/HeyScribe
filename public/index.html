Transcription Made Easy

Transcription is something that's very easy to underestimate. HeyScribe attempts to bridge the gap between clients and transcriptionists (scribes). In an attempt to make things a little more painless, we have a robust process through which our scribes are approved, with all manner of jobs so that each scribe can find the right job for them. From lectures in need of subtitles to professional cinematic transcriptions, HeyScribe exists as a marketplace where clients with infinite needs can meet scribes with infinite skills.

Faster Results

Thanks to our emphasis on fast, precise work, our turnarounds are much faster than our competitors. This is thanks to a few different factors: precise expectations, simple avenues of communication, and expertise on all sides. We're in the business of getting people to what they love, as soon as possible.

Quality Work

HeyScribe has a robust credential verification system that is completely optional, but perfect for clientss that need precise and perfect work done every time. In return, scribes that have gone through the verification process will be eligible to take higher-scale projects, which are some of the most fun and exciting opportunities available in the market.

On Your Terms

Nobody is forced to take a job at HeyScribe. Unlike most transcription firms, there's no requirement in terms of volume or duration; a two second clip has every opportunity that a ten hour epic does, and a scribe won't be forced to take either of them. In this way we've ensured that everyone involved feels heard and valued. After all, if you don't like a job, don't take it!
